# 🎬 YouTube Production Autopilot V3.0

## 🔄 THE CORE LOOP
**STEP 0: Conceptualize**
- Use `brainstorming` to propose **3 unique angles**.
- **MANDATE:** Define Audience Profile & Core Soul (Silent Pain or Straw Man).
- **PAUSE:** Confirm Angle with User.

**STEP 1: Deep Research**
- Use `youtube-deep-researcher V1.1`.
- **RESEARCH:** 5-10 sources (Mixed: .edu, .gov, Expert, Reddit).
- **CONSOLIDATION:** Prepare Research Brief. STOP for approval.

**STEP 2: Non-Lossy Staged Writing**
Loop `youtube-script-master V8.1` for 1500+ words:
- **Phase A:** Chapters 1-2 (~800 words).
- **Phase B:** Chapters 3-4 (~800 words).
- **Phase C:** Chapter 5 + Assets (Dual Titles, Pinned Comment, Shorts/Comm).

**STEP 3: Assets & Audit**
- `youtube-visual-prompt` for Thumbnails/Storyboard.
- `youtube-script-quality-auditor` for final check.

## 🚨 MANDATES (Non-Negotiable)
1. **The 1-3 Rule:** Research 10 sources, but explicitly cite **MAX 3** in the script.
2. **Inner Monologue:** Pierce the soul with a deep question every 2-3 mins.
3. **Non-Lossy Packaging:** Cleanup MUST NOT compress or summarize approved dialogue.
4. **Length:** Minimum 1500 words for long-form.
