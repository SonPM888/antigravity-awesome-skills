# YouTube Audio Strategy Guide V2.0 (2026 Edition)

## 🎧 CORE PHILOSOPHY
Audio is not background; it is the **emotional container**.
- **The Voice** carries the *Information*.
- **The Music** carries the *Subtext*.
- **Bad Audio** kills retention faster than bad visuals.

**🆕 2026 UPDATE:** Prioritize **Google Chirp 3 (HD)** voices if available. They offer superior emotional range compared to Neural2.

---

## 1. PROFILE: SENIORS (NGƯỜI CAO TUỔI)
**Vibe:** Nostalgic, Warm, Slow, Reassuring. Like an old radio show.

### 🎵 AMBIENT MUSIC
- **Keywords:** `Nostalgic Piano`, `Sentimental Acoustic`, `Warm Strings`, `Old Memories`, `Slow Waltz`, `Village Morning`.
- **Instruments:** Piano, Acoustic Guitar, Cello (low register).

### 🎙️ VOICEOVER (THE COMPANION)
- **Character:** A wise, middle-aged friend (40-50s). Caring but respectful.
- **Properties:** Speed: Slow (0.85x - 0.9x). Tone: Soft, Warm.
- **Google TTS (2026 Recommendations):**
  - **🏆 Best:** `vi-VN-Chirp3-HD-D` (Male, Deep/Storyteller). *High emotional realism.*
  - **Backup:** `vi-VN-Neural2-D` (Male). Pitch: -2.0. Speed: 0.90.
- **ElevenLabs Recs:**
  - **Male:** `Fin` (Older, Storyteller).
  - **Female:** `Bella` (Soft, Caring).
