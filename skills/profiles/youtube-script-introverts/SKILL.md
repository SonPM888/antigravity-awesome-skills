## 📋 PHASE-BY-PHASE OVERRIDES (V6.0 Compatible)

### PHASE 2: THE H.S.O. OPENING (Override)
- **Rule:** Start with a sensory overload trigger vs. relief.
- **Formula:** "You are at a party. The music is too loud. Someone is asking you what you do for a living. And all you want... is to leave."
- **Visual:** Chaotic noise -> Sudden silence (The Hook).

### PHASE 4: THE MASTER VOICESCRIPT (THE INNER OBSERVATION Override)

Every 500-word chunk must execute the **4-Point Quiet Check**:

1. **Internal Monologue:** Use thoughts (T) more than speech (S).
   - ✅ Example: "Bạn gật đầu cười, nhưng trong đầu bạn đang đếm ngược từng phút để về nhà."

2. **Sensory Sensitivity:** Describe the overwhelm of "too much."
   - ✅ Example: "Tiếng cười nói rộn ràng nghe như tiếng búa khoan vào màng nhĩ."

3. **Validation of Boundaries:** Saying "No" is a superpower.
   - ✅ Example: "Từ chối một cuộc nhậu không phải là kiêu ngạo. Đó là bảo vệ năng lượng."

4. **Reframing Weakness:** Silence = Analysis.
   - ✅ Example: "Họ nghĩ bạn nhút nhát. Thực ra bạn đang quan sát từng cử chỉ của họ."

**Metaphor Engine (Quiet Strength):**
1. **The Battery:** It drains fast but holds high voltage.
2. **The Filter:** You feel everything; that's why you need to shield yourself.
3. **The Iceberg:** 90% of you is underwater (internal).
4. **The Sniper:** You speak rarely, but when you do, you don't miss.

**Sensory Focus (Contrast: Chaos vs. Peace):**
- **Chaos:** Neon lights, overlapping voices, sticky floors, phone notifications pinging.
- **Peace:** The click of a door locking, the weight of a blanket, the sound of rain, dim lamp light.
