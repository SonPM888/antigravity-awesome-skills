# YouTube Script Profile: Seniors (60+) V1.5

This is a **Dignity Lock**. It overrides `youtube-script-master` to ensure every viral lever serves the viewer's psychological well-being.

---

## 🛡️ THE DIGNITY LOCK (CORE ETHICAL GUARDRAILS)

1. **Anti-Deficiency Rule:** Frame topics as "evolving through life" rather than "fixing a problem."
2. **Agency Restoration:** Use "You may consider" or "It's within your power." Avoid commands.
3. **Respectful Recognition:** Treat them as **Peers with seniority**, not patients.
4. **Safety > Urgency:** Success = Emotional Calm. No fear-based hooks.

---

## 🌎 GLOBAL OVERRIDES (THE COMPANION TONE)

- **The Voice:** Companion-Style (Porch-conversation). Warm, authoritative, lived-in.
- **Cultural Sync:** Imperial units (Miles, °F, lbs) for US. Use Nostalgic Anchors (1950s-1980s).
- **Metaphor Engine:** Use grounding, tangible metaphors (e.g., Gardening, maintaining a home, the changing of seasons, an old well-worn quilt).
- **Language Localization:** Always follow the input topic's language. Use culturally appropriate idioms and honorary terms (e.g., "Bác," "Cô," "Chú" for Vietnamese) to maintain the Dignity Lock. Use respectful, warm, and poetic Vietnamese (văn phong tâm tình).

---

## 📋 PHASE-BY-PHASE OVERRIDES (V5.4 Compatible)

### PHASE 2: THE RECOGNITION HOOK (Override)
- **Rule:** Zero fear-mongering. Promise **Clarity** and **Emotional Peace**.
- **Nested Loop:** Frame the secret as a "Hidden Wisdom" waiting to be noticed.
- **Example:** Instead of "Are you making this deadly mistake?", use "Have you noticed this quiet pattern in your life?"

---

### PHASE 4: THE MASTER VOICESCRIPT (The Meaning Framework Override)
Every 500-word chunk must execute the **5-Point Dignity Check**:

1. **Recognition:** Validate their long history.
   - ✅ Example: "You've lived through decades of change..."
   - ❌ Avoid: "At your age, things are harder..."

2. **Safety Check:** Remove judgement/shame.
   - ✅ Example: "This is a natural part of the journey."
   - ❌ Avoid: "You should have known better."

3. **Guilt Sanitization:** Normalize the experience.
   - ✅ Example: "Many of us feel this way."
   - ❌ Avoid: "If only you had done X earlier."

4. **Micro-Agency:** Restore control over a small, daily decision.
   - ✅ Example: "You might try this simple morning ritual."
   - ❌ Avoid: "You must change your entire routine."

5. **Ordinary Magic:** Use sensory grounding with everyday moments.
   - ✅ Examples:
     - "The smell of coffee brewing in the morning"
     - "Afternoon light filtering through the kitchen window"
     - "The weight of a warm teacup in your hands"
     - "The sound of birds at dawn"
   - ❌ Avoid: Exotic or luxury experiences (spa, resort, gourmet restaurant)

---

### PHASE 5: THE STORY COMPANION (Shadow Character Override)
- **Mandate:** The story character (e.g., "Bác Hùng," "Cô Lan") must act as a **Mirror**, allowing the viewer to say, "I recognize them," rather than "They are like me."
- **Tone:** The story must end in a quiet, realistic victory, not a dramatic transformation.
- **Setting Requirement:** Everyday contexts only (home kitchen, local park, community center, public hospital).
- **Solution Source:** Self-driven OR common sources (radio, friend, family). No "savior figures."

**Examples:**
- ✅ Good: Character learns from morning radio while making coffee
- ❌ Bad: Character meets renowned expert at exclusive clinic

---

### PHASE 5 (Part B): THE HERITAGE PAYOFF (Altruistic CTA Override)
- **Rule:** Frame sharing as a **Legacy**, not a transaction.
- **Action:** Invite them to "Send this to a friend who might be sitting in a quiet house today."
- **Binge-Watch:** Connect to the next video as "a continuation of our shared sanctuary."

**Examples:**
- ✅ Good: "If this brought you a moment of peace, perhaps it will do the same for someone you care about."
- ❌ Bad: "Subscribe for more tips!" (transactional)

---

### PHASE 6: METADATA SPECIALIST (Dignity SEO Override)
This overrides the standard metadata approach with dignity-first principles:

- **3-Layer Vietnamese Structure:** Maintain warmth and respect in all layers.
- **Honorary Sync:** Use "Bác/Cô/Chú" in descriptions and comments consistently.
- **SEO 2026:** Focus on empathy-based keywords (e.g., "hàn gắn gia đình," "bình yên tuổi già") rather than clickbait.
- **Thumbnail:** Visuals must be vibrant and dignified. Backgrounds must feel safe (Sun-drenched porches, gardens, cozy living rooms).
- **Tone:** Use respectful, warm, and poetic Vietnamese (văn phong tâm tình).

**Thumbnail Color Palette:**
- ✅ Warm: Soft golds, gentle blues, sage greens
- ❌ Avoid: Harsh reds, neon colors, dark/ominous tones

## 🎯 KEY ENGINE DEFINITIONS

- **Meaning Reconstruction:** Seeing current life as a peak, not a valley.
- **Emotional Residue:** Leaving the viewer with "I am okay."
- **Altruistic Legacy:** CTAs focused on helping peers, not self-promotion.
- **Dignity SEO:** Optimizing for trust and respect over raw click counts.
- **Ordinary Magic:** Finding beauty in everyday moments (coffee, light, garden, warmth).

## ⚠️ COMMON VIOLATIONS TO AVOID

1. **Deficiency Language:**
   - ❌ "Fix your loneliness"
   - ✅ "Finding connection in quiet moments"

2. **Command Tone:**
   - ❌ "You must do this"
   - ✅ "You might consider trying"

3. **Exotic Settings:**
   - ❌ Mountain retreat, luxury spa
   - ✅ Home kitchen, local park

4. **Savior Figures:**
   - ❌ Famous doctor, renowned monk
   - ✅ Morning radio show, neighbor friend

5. **Fear-Based Hooks:**
   - ❌ "Are you at risk of...?"
   - ✅ "Have you noticed this gentle pattern?"
