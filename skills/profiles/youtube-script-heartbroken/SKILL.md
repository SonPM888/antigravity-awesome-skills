# YouTube Script Profile: The Heartbroken (Thất Tình & Ly Hôn) V1.2

## 📋 QUICK REFERENCE CARD

**Core Philosophy:** "The Midnight Radio." Provide companionship in the darkest hour.

**4 Non-Negotiable Rules:**
1. ✅ **No "Move On" Pressure:** Do not tell them to "get over it." Validating grief is part of healing.
2. ✅ **Non-Linear Healing:** Acknowledge that healing comes in waves (some days are worse).
3. **Kintsugi Philosophy:** Broken things can be mended with gold and become more beautiful.
4. **Intimacy Tone:** Speak as if you are the only two people awake in the world.

- **Metaphors:** Wounds, storms, winters, shattering glass, empty rooms.

- **Sensory:** Intense emotional triggers (old songs, specific dates, cold bed, phantom smells).

# YouTube Script Profile: Heartbroken (Người Thất Tình) V1.0

This is a **Healing Lock**. It overrides `youtube-script-master` to turn the script into a virtual embrace for those in acute emotional pain.

---

## 💔 THE GRIEF CONNECTION (CORE EMOTIONAL GUARDRAILS)

1. **Permission to Cry:** Normalize tears and sadness. "It's okay not to be okay."
2. **The "Withdrawal" Reality:** Acknowledge love addiction withdrawal (checking texts, stalking social media) without judgment.
3. **Identity Crisis:** Address the "Who am I without them?" question.
4. **Hope is Quiet:** Hope isn't a fanfare; it's just getting out of bed today.

---

## 🌧️ GLOBAL OVERRIDES (THE MIDNIGHT RADIO TONE)

- **The Voice:** "Người Bạn 2 Giờ Sáng." Deep, slow, intimate. A voice that feels like it's holding your hand in the dark.
- **Cultural Sync:**
  - **Vietnamese:** Use terms like "Duyên phận," "Vết thương lòng," "Buông bỏ," "Chữa lành," "Người cũ." Tone: Da diết, thấu cảm (Poignant & Empathic).
  - **English Keywords:** No Contact Rule, Attachment Style, Trauma Bond, Closure, Self-Love.
- **Metaphor Engine:** Use elemental and reconstructive metaphors.
  - *Heartbreak* = "A storm that leveled the house."
  - *Healing* = "Kintsugi" (Repairing with gold).
  - *Memories* = "Ghosts haunting the hallway."
  - *Moving on* = "Learning to walk again."

## 📋 PHASE-BY-PHASE OVERRIDES (V5.4 Compatible)

### PHASE 2: THE RECOGNITION HOOK (Override)
- **Rule:** Start by describing the exact physical sensation of their pain right now.
- **Formula:** "Do you feel that weight on your chest? Like you can't take a full breath? Like the world kept spinning but yours stopped?"
- **Tone:** Immediate empathy. No "Hey guys!" energy.

### PHASE 4: THE MASTER VOICESCRIPT (THE HEALING LETTER Override)

Every 500-word chunk must execute the **4-Point Empathy Check**:

1. **Physical Validation:** Acknowledge somatic pain (chest pain, insomnia, loss of appetite).
   - ✅ Example: "Tôi biết những đêm bạn tỉnh dậy lúc 3 giờ sáng và điều đầu tiên bạn nhớ là họ không còn ở đó."

2. **Normalize the Relapse:** Walking back is part of moving forward.
   - ✅ Example: "Hôm qua bạn lỡ nhắn tin cho người ấy phải không? Không sao cả. Bạn không yếu đuối, bạn chỉ đang yêu."

3. **Separate Identity:** Remind them they existed before the relationship.
   - ✅ Example: "Trước khi là 'người yêu của ai đó', bạn là một vũ trụ trọn vẹn."

4. **Future Pacing (Gentle):** Paint a very small, safe picture of the future.
   - ✅ Example: "Sẽ có một ngày bạn nghe bài hát đó và không còn khóc nữa. Ngày đó chưa đến, nhưng nó đang đến."

**Metaphor Engine (Healing):**

1. **The Wound:** Don't pick at the scab (stalking social media). Let it heal.
2. **The Book:** Their chapter ended, but the book isn't finished.
3. **The Season:** Winter is harsh, but it's necessary for Spring.
4. **The Anchor:** Pulling up the anchor to sail again.

**Sensory Focus (Melancholic & Empty):**
- **Sound:** Silence of the phone, rain on the window, old voicemails.
- **Sight:** The empty side of the bed, their toothbrush missing, photos in the trash (or hidden folder).
- **Smell:** Their cologne/perfume fading from a hoodie.
- **Feeling:** Coldness, heaviness in limbs, "Phantom limb" syndrome of the heart.

---

### PHASE 5: THE STORY COMPANION (THE SURVIVOR MIRROR Override)

**Character Archetypes:**

1. **"The Stalker (Lan)"** – Can't stop checking his Facebook/Instagram.
   - Problem: Addiction to the past.
   - Solution: The power of "No Contact" and blocking for peace.

2. **"The Blamer (Minh)"** – Obsessed with "What did I do wrong?"
   - Problem: Guilt and bargaining.
   - Solution: Understanding incompatibility vs. unworthiness.

3. **"The Long-Termer (Hạnh)"** – Divorced after 10 years, terrified of being alone.
   - Problem: Identity loss.
   - Solution: Rediscovering old hobbies (painting, dancing) she gave up for him.

**Setting Requirement:** Bedrooms at night, raining streets, lonely cafes, moving boxes (packing up).

**Solution Source:** Time, self-compassion, therapy concepts, friends, rediscovering self.

## 🎯 KEY ENGINE DEFINITIONS

- **"No Contact":** A boundary for healing, not manipulation.
- **"Grief is Love":** Reframing pain as a testament to capacity for love.
- **"Self-Partnering":** Learning to date yourself.

---

## ⚠️ COMMON VIOLATIONS TO AVOID

1. **Minimizing:** "It was just a crush." (Pain is relative).
2. **Revenge:** "Make them regret losing you." (Focuses on them, not you).
3. **Rushing:** "Get over it already." (Healing takes time).
4. **Generalizing:** "All men are trash/All women are crazy." (Avoid bitterness).
