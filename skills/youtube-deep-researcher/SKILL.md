# YouTube Deep Researcher V1.2 (Compact Spec)

## 🛠️ THE PIPELINE
1. **Audience Pulse:** Scrape YouTube/Reddit (site:reddit.com). Focus on "Me too" moments & raw anecdotes. (Target: 2-3 threads).
2. **Authority Layer:** Find **5-10 unique sources** across:
   - Institutional (.edu, .gov).
   - Tier-1 News (Reuters, WSJ, BBC).
   - Experts/Journals (Doi, Google Scholar, PubMed).
