### 1:30 - CHAPTER 2: THE VILLAIN & THE PAPER TIGER (ANALOGY)
**(Analogy Mandate: The Parasite)**

### 3:30 - CHAPTER 3: THE TURNING POINT (MODULE D: THE JUSTICE ARC)

Thay vì viết: "Doanh số tăng 20%."
